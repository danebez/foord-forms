HOW TO INVEST WITH FOORD

  1. Complete all relevant sections in this form, print it and sign where required. If you require any assistance, please call us on 021 531 5085.
  2. Fax the form together with the required supporting documentation and proof of the deposit and a cancelled cheque or recent bank statement to Foord Unit Trusts on 021 532 6970, or e-mail to unittrusts@foord.co.za.
  3. Post the original form and supporting documentation to Foord Unit Trusts at PO Box 135, Howard Place, 7450.
  4. Please note that the business cut-off time for receiving instructions is 14h00.
