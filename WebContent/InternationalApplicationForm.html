HOW TO PURCHASE FOORD INTERNATIONAL TRUST UNITS
(DIRECT OFFSHORE INVESTMENT)

  1. Complete all relevant sections in this form, print it and sign where required. If you require any assistance, please call us on 021 531 5085.
  2. South African investors should fax the form together with the required supporting documentation and proof of the deposit and a cancelled cheque or recent bank statement to Foord Unit Trusts on 021 532 6970 or e-mail to unittrusts@foord.co.za, and post the original form and supporting documentation to Foord Unit Trusts at PO Box 135, Howard Place, 7450. Foord will courier these documents to Guernsey.
  3. All other investors should post the original form and supporting documentation to Foord Asset Management (Guernsey) Limited, PO Box 105, Trafalgar Court, Admiral Park, St Peter Port, Guernsey, Channel Islands, GY1 2HT.

Notes:

  • The Foord International Trust trades weekly. In order to comply with the Guernsey cut-off time of 16h00 GMT each Monday, instructions must reach Foord Unit Trusts by 14h00 on Friday.
  • Owing to Guernsey regulations, your investment will be activated on receipt of cash and faxed instructions but will be frozen until original documentation is received.
  • International settlements can take up to 48 hours. In order to qualify for the Guernsey cut-off time, your deposit must have settled into the Guernsey bank accounts by 16h00 on a Monday.
  • In order to effect an international transfer from South Africa, your bank will require a valid tax clearance certificate from you.
  • Anti-money laundering regulations in Guernsey prohibit money transfers from third parties.
