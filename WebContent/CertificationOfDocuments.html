CERTIFICATION OF DOCUMENTS

Copies should be certified as correct copies of the original by a respected professional person (i.e. a notary public, a lawyer, accountant or banker), using substantially the following form of certification:

"I [name of person], hereby certify that the copy of the identification documentation attached hereto is a true and correct copy of the original document produced to me."

The certifier should sign and date the copy document (printing his or her name clearly underneath) and clearly indicate the position of capacity on it together with a contact phone number.
